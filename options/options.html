ABOUT Unv.is Plus

This browser extension use the service provided by Unv.is, a web proxy tool for stripping away all fancy stuff (like the Readability feature in Firefox) from web pages into a simple readable text-format of almost any web page.

DISCLAIMER: I'm not part of the www.unv.is project/team. For questions regarding service & functionality, pages that wont filter correctly etc, then contact unv.is for help.

HOW IT WORKS

  1. Right-click on a link
  2. Select Unv.is this link... from the popup menu
  3. Enjoy!

Damn! Is that it!? Yup, I know, it's magic, right? When you've mastered the art of right-clicking links, you're ready to unleash the full power and glory of this mighty godlike extension. Click on the SETUP-tab above, build your own list of customized URLs and your browser will automatically unv.is those pages for you. No questions asked!

Pattern Matches
*://*.example.org/*

Match all HTTP and HTTPS URLs that are hosted at "example.org" or one of its subdomains.

 http://www.example.org/
http://www.example.org/some/path/
https://some.domain.org/path/
*://*.example.org/article/*

Match all HTTP and HTTPS URLs that are hosted at "example.org" or one of its subdomains WITH article/ as path.

 http://www.example.org/article/
http://www.example.org/article/a/
https://a.domain.net/article/
*://example.com/*

Match all HTTP and HTTPS URLs that are hosted at exactly "example.com/".

 http://example.com/
https://example.com/
